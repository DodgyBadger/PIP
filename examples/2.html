Aurora X2 Portable Smart Hub

Battery-friendly automation hub combining multi-radio flexibility with a lightweight rules engine for edge workflows.

Key details

  • Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.3, and Zigbee bridge support
  • Quad-core ARM with 4 GB LPDDR4X memory
  • Encrypted local storage with secure boot
  • Optional remote access tunnel with granular audit logs

Deployment notes

Ships preloaded with diagnostics and a guided onboarding flow. Designed to survive brownouts using its internal battery and power-loss recovery.
