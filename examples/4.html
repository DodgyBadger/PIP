Aurora X2 Portable Smart Hub

The Aurora X2 is a compact, battery-friendly smart hub designed for home offices and small studios. It consolidates connectivity, local automation, and telemetry into a single device that fits in the palm of your hand.

At a glance

  • Wi‑Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.3 connectivity
  • Local automations with edge compute (quad‑core ARM)
  • Silent, fanless design with passive cooling
  • USB‑C PD 30W input, 10,000 mAh internal battery
  • End‑to‑end encrypted remote access

Overview

Aurora X2 brings reliable connectivity and automation to spaces where desktops and rack gear are overkill. Pair sensors, cameras, and peripherals, and orchestrate routines that run locally—even when your internet connection drops. The on‑device scheduler and rules engine minimize cloud calls, keeping latency low and data private.

Setup takes minutes: connect power, join your Wi‑Fi network, and walk through the guided pairing flow. A built‑in diagnostics panel helps troubleshoot interference and channel congestion without extra tools.

FAQ

Does Aurora X2 require a cloud account?

No. Core features work offline. Remote access and backups are optional and can be disabled entirely.

Can I power it from a laptop charger?

Yes, any USB‑C PD charger capable of 18 W or more will work. For best results, use the included cable.

How many devices can I connect?

Tested with up to 64 concurrent BLE peripherals and 20 Wi‑Fi clients. Actual limits depend on your environment.

Compliance and safety

CE, FCC, and UKCA certified. Operating temperature 0–40 °C. For indoor use only. See the safety leaflet for battery handling guidelines.
