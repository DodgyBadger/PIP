Aurora X2 Portable Smart Hub

Designed for resilient automation, Aurora X2 balances silent hardware, flexible radios, and encrypted state sync.

Industrial design

CNC milled shell with passive cooling fins and rubberized base to stay stable in rack shelves or desktop mounts.

Interfaces

  • 2× USB-A 3.0, 1× USB-C PD, 1× HDMI micro
  • Expandable GPIO header for sensor buses
  • Wi-Fi 6, BLE 5.3, optional LoRa module
